All about Raspberry Pi!

The Raspberry Pi is a credit card-sized single-board computer developed by the Raspberry Pi Foundation in the UK. The initial goal of the device was to promote and teach basic computer science in developing countries. The firstr model released in February of 2012 and the most recent model (the Raspberry Pi 3 model B) was released in February of 2016.

Specifications and uses of the Raspberry Pi

The Raspberry Pi was developed to promote the teaching of computer science in developing countries and has been used as a teaching tool throughout the world. The Pi is used for entry level computer science pros and amateurs alike for its compact size and range of application. Often, the Pi has been seen as a possible supplement for traditional PCs, but not as a direct replacement.
